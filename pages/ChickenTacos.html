Chicken Tacos

Description

Delicious and customizable, chicken tacos are a popular Mexican dish consisting of seasoned chicken served in soft or hard tortillas, topped with various fillingsâ€‹.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast or thighs, diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 packet taco seasoning or homemade spice mix
  • 8 small tortillas (soft or hard) (adjust to taste)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Toppings: diced onions, chopped cilantro, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, sour cream, salsa, avocado slices

Steps

  1. Cook the Chicken: Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the diced chicken and cook until browned and cooked through. Sprinkle with taco seasoning and a bit of water; stir to coat. Let it simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens.

  2. Warm the Tortillas: Heat tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until warm and pliable.

  3. Assemble the Tacos: Place a portion of the seasoned chicken onto each tortilla. Add desired toppings.

  4. Serve: Fold the tortillas and serve immediately.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

A beloved dessert, these chocolate chip cookies are soft, chewy, and packed with rich chocolate flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (226g) unsalted butter, softened​
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3/4 cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup (165g) packed brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 1/4 cups (280g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups (340g) semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add to the butter-sugar mixture, mixing until just combined.

  3. Add Chocolate Chips: In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add to the butter-sugar mixture, mixing until just combined.

  4. Shape the Cookies: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper. Roll the dough into balls, about 1.5 inches in diameter, and place them on the baking sheets, leaving space between them

  5. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are set and slightly golden. The centers may look underbaked but will firm up as they cool.

  6. Cool: Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.

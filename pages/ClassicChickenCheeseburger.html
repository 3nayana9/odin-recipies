Classic Chicken Cheeseburger

Description

A lighter twist on the traditional cheeseburger, this chicken version is flavorful, juicy, and topped with melted cheese and classic burger fixings.

Ingredients

  • 250g ground chicken (or shredded cooked chicken)
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 burger buns
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar or processed)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • Onion rings (optional)
  • Ketchup, mayonnaise, or mustard

Steps

  1. Make the patties: In a bowl, mix ground chicken, breadcrumbs, egg, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Form into 2 round burger patties.

  2. Cook the patties: Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat. Cook the patties for 4â€“5 minutes on each side, until golden brown and cooked through.

  3. Melt the cheese In the last minute of cooking, place a cheese slice on each patty and cover the pan to let the cheese melt.

  4. Toast the buns: Slice the burger buns in half and toast them lightly in the same pan for extra flavor.

  5. Assemble the burger: Spread mayonnaise, ketchup, or mustard on the bottom bun. Add lettuce, cheesy chicken patty, tomato slices, onion rings (if using), and top with the other half of the bun.

  6. Serve hot: Enjoy your classic chicken cheeseburger with fries or a salad!

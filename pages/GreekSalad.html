Greek Salad

Description

A traditional Greek salad, known as Horiatiki, is a refreshing and vibrant dish that showcases the best of Mediterranean flavors. It's a staple in Greek cuisine, especially during the warmer months, and is perfect as a side dish or a light meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 large ripe tomatoes, cut into wedges
  • 1 cucumber, peeled, deseeded, and chopped
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup Kalamata olives
  • 200g feta cheese, cut into large blocks
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the Vegetables: In a large bowl, combine the chopped tomatoes, cucumber, sliced red onion, and green bell pepper.

  2. Add Olives and Feta: Add the Kalamata olives and place the blocks of feta cheese on top of the vegetables.

  3. Season: Sprinkle the dried oregano over the salad. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.

  4. Serve: Drizzle the extra virgin olive oil over the salad. If using, add the red wine vinegar.

  5. Toss and Serve: Gently toss the salad to combine all the ingredients. Serve immediately, ideally with crusty bread to soak up the flavorful juices.

source: The Mediterranean dish

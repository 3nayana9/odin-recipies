Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish from Naples, Spaghetti Aglio e Olio combines spaghetti with a simple sauce made from garlic, olive oil, and chili flakes. It's known for its bold flavors and quick preparation.​

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for serving)

Steps

  1. Cook the Spaghetti: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve about 1 cup of pasta cooking water before draining.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-low heat. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Sauté gently, stirring frequently, until the garlic turns golden brown. Be careful not to burn it.

  3. Combine Pasta and Sauce: Add the drained spaghetti to the skillet. Toss to coat the pasta in the garlic-infused oil. If the mixture seems dry, add a bit of the reserved pasta water to achieve a silky consistency.

  4. Serve: Garnish with chopped parsley and grated Parmesan cheese if desired. Serve immediately.
